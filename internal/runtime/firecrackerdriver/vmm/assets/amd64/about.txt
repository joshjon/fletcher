This directory holds the bundled Firecracker VMM binary and guest kernel for
linux/amd64:

  firecracker   the Firecracker VMM (from the firecracker-microvm releases)
  vmlinux       a minimal guest kernel (from the firecracker CI artifacts)

Both files are gitignored and fetched by `make fetch-vmm`. They are embedded into
the fletcher binary at build time and extracted to a cache directory on first
run. This about.txt is committed so the `//go:embed assets/amd64` directive
compiles on a fresh checkout before the binaries are fetched.

Versions are pinned in scripts/fetch-vmm.sh / the Makefile.
